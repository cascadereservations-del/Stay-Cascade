Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking direct.

What time is check-in and check-out?

Check-in begins at 2:00 PM. Check-out is at 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in or late check-out may be arranged depending on availability — just mention it in your booking request and we’ll do our best.

How do I confirm my booking?

Once you submit the booking form, you’ll receive an Acknowledgement Receipt (AR) by email within 1–2 hours. Your stay is secured when the 50% deposit clears. Balance is settled at check-in — no surprises.

What payment methods are accepted?

GCash (0956 011 5744 · Marifel Suzanne B.), UnionBank transfer (1094 2361 1134), or cash on arrival. All payments go directly to the property — there is no third-party processor.

Is there free parking?

Yes. Bria Homes is a gated subdivision with 24/7 security. Parking is available inside the village at no extra charge.

What is the WiFi like?

The unit has fiber broadband. It handles remote work, video calls, and streaming simultaneously without issue.

Can I have visitors during my stay?

Day visitors are welcome. We only ask that you let us know in advance. Overnight guests beyond the declared headcount are not permitted.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations 7 or more days before check-in receive a full refund. 3–6 days notice: 50% of the deposit is returned. Under 3 days: the booking is fully honoured in place.

Is this an Airbnb listing?

Yes — Cascade Hideaway is a verified Airbnb property (4.97★ · 34 reviews · Guest Favorite). Booking here directly gives you the exact same property at a lower total cost, with payment going directly to the host. You can verify the listing on Airbnb before booking if that gives you peace of mind.

What's included in every stay?

Every stay includes hotel-grade linens and towels, a fully equipped kitchen, fiber broadband fast enough for remote work and streaming, a 43” Smart TV, a dedicated workspace, and a stand fan. Cleaning is always included in the rate — there is no separate cleaning charge added at checkout. Self check-in via smart lock means your arrival happens entirely on your own schedule.

Longer stays include a complimentary mid-stay room refresh (5+ nights) and priority response on any request during your visit. This is a boutique property managed by a real host — not a large operator — so you have a direct line to someone who cares.

Do returning guests get any exclusive rates?

This site exists for guests who prefer a direct relationship with the host. For those planning longer stays — or returning for another visit — we offer loyalty rates that reflect that relationship. These are applied automatically when you book here; no codes, no negotiation needed.
